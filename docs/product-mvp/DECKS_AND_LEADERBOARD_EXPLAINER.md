# BitPod Decks + Tower Explainer (User-Facing)

## What BitPod is (in one sentence)
BitPod is a Bitcoin “regime radar” with a canonical Baseline engine and optional Decks you can tune—always showing how your Deck differs from default.

## Baseline comes first
Before public Decks/Tower become meaningful, the Baseline must prove itself via forward-scored predictions under published rules.

## Key terms
- **Baseline**: default BitPod (“factory settings”), always the reference.
- **Deck**: your tuned configuration/preset.
- **Personalization Delta**: what changed vs baseline and by how much.
- **Tower**: ranks Decks by forward-scored performance over time.

## Antenna Arrays
Podcasts surface “look here” signals. They do not verify truth.

## Zaps (optional later)
Tips can support creators/compute. Ranking remains performance-based.

## The promise
If BitPod can’t prove it, it won’t sell it.
