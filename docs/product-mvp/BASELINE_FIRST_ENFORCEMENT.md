# Baseline First: Enforcement Policy

## Statement
No public deck ecosystem until baseline credibility is demonstrated under published scoring rules.

## Blocked until baseline passes
- public Tower ranking
- public Deck library
- social reputation features
- “creator economy” mechanics (zaps, badges)
- shareable “Top deck” claims

## Allowed before baseline passes
- internal/private Deck experiments
- internal scoring + backtesting + forward testing
- verification pipeline hardening
- UX prototypes that do not imply predictive authority

## Why this policy exists
If baseline is weak:
- personalization becomes overfitting theater
- rankings reward noise
- social features amplify false confidence
- the product becomes a meme generator, not an intelligence system

Baseline credibility is the keystone. Everything else hangs from it.
